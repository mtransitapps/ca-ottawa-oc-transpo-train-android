Full description:
This application adds Ottawa OC Transpo O-Train information to MonTransit BETA.

This app contains the bus schedule (offline and real-time) and the latest news from www.octranspo1.com and @OC_Transpo and @OCTranspoLive on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

OC Transpo serves Ottawa in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display O-Train information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by OC Transpo and the City of Ottawa
http://www.octranspo1.com/developers
http://data.ottawa.ca/en/dataset/oc-transpo-schedules

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-ottawa-oc-transpo-train-android

This app is not related with OC Transpo.

Permissions:
- Other: required to load OC Transpo Live Next Bus Arrivals & read news from www.octranspo1.com and Twitter.
